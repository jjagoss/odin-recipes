Greek Salad

Description

The true origin of the ever popular Greek Salad is unknown, but it is a refreshing classic to be sure. A chopped salad of cucumber, bellpeppers, red onions, and above all tomatoes, this salad is complemented by its dijon and red wine vinegar based dressing before it's topped with unctuous olives and a dry crumbled feta.

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Green bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • English cucumber
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Dijon mustard
  • Garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Fresh mint
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. To make the dressing: Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, dijon mustard, one diced garlic clove, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  2. To make the salad: Roughly chop tomatoes, bell pepper, red onion, and English cucumber and place chopped vegetables in a bowl along with olives.
  3. Top salad with dressing and feta crumbles.
  4. Toss and enjoy.
